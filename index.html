Our purpose

From April to October of each year, Berrien County teems with Mexicans who come to work a seasonal agricultural job. We focus on teaching the Word of God to these Migrant families so that they might hear the Gospel, believe on the Lord Jesus, and be discipled in their new life with Christ.
