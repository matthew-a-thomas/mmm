How you can be involved

  1. Pray for more laborers so that we can expand and reach more migrant camps
  2. Pray for the children to grow in their walk with God
  3. Pray for us to have opportunities to witness to the parents. We need wisdom in how to arrange visits in between their hectic schedule
  4. Pray for us to have wisdom in discipling the children
  5. Pray for the salvation of the farmers
  6. Donate classroom supplies (paper, markers, stickers)
  7. Volunteer to help with Day Camp as a bus driver, cook, activity director, bus aide, maintenance man, teacher, teacher assistant (counselor)
  8. Adopt a child for prayer
  9. Adopt a farm for prayer
  10. Volunteer to help during the despedida (1st Saturday of October)
  11. Attend a 5 day club as a helper

4. Dress code for workers

Be willing to abide by this dress code for working with Michigan Migrant Ministries:

Ladies

  • Pants, jeans, and capris are permitted during and after Camp hours but MUST fit these guidelines:
    1. They must be loose enough around the legs that you can easily “pinch an inch” of fabric on the outside of the leg without having to stretch or pull the fabric
    2. They must not “hug” the buttocks. If you don‘t have any pants/capris that fit these guidelines, please wear a skirt or culottes
  • Skirts/culottes must cover the knees when sitting. Please wear leggings if you wear a skirt
  • Shorts are not permitted
  • Other shirts may be worn after Camp hours but cannot show any cleavage. No tank tops, spaghetti strap, sleeveless or tight fitting shirts permitted. If in doubt, leave it at home!

Men

  • Shorts are allowed during and after Camp hours but they must cover your knees when sitting
  • Tank tops and sleeveless shirts are not permitted

All workers

  • Camp T-shirt must be worn during Camp hours
  • Please abide by the above dress code when
    1. Arriving and departing from Camp
    2. Attending Staff Meetings prior to Camp
    3. Making visits to the migrant families in the farms
    4. Attending local Church services

Thank you for adhering to these guidelines while working with the children and families during Camp!
